Alpaca Kit instructions.

Step 1: Gather Your Components
  • 1 x 2032 Battery
  • 1 x Metal Battery Clip
  • 1 x 3mm LED
  • 1 x Switch
  • 1 x Cotton Ball
First step lay the PCB flat and soldering a small hill on the middle solder pad. Make sure the hill is shallow and has no sharp parts. Wait for the PCB to cool and then hold it up if you can see the bump from the side it is tall enough. Place the switch into the location as shown, it has two plastic tabs that should fit in flatly into the pcb. Solder each one of the tabs. Next is the battery clip, this is tricky and will take pushing a fair amount of heat into the pcb so its important to use the helping hands. Use one of the clips to help hold the battery clip in position, have the other clip hold the pcb, and have the PCB touching the table to make 3 point of contacts for a more secure platform. Solder one side, it doesnt need to be perfect just enough to hold it. It should look something like this. In the picture you will see that it looks like some solder has gone under the plate and is coming out of the corner. That means there is a layer]
